about us

Amor's Catering

Welcome to Plaza Catering, a place where every celebration holds a place in our hearts. Our journey began on June 16 2003 in the town of pollachi, Tamilnadu. Starting as a catering unit we are now proud to be known for our exceptional services that cater to your every need and create unforgettable experiences for your esteemed guests.

Being the best catering services in Kerala, we offer more than delicious food; we provide a complete orchestra of services. From catering and venue decoration to event management we ensure that every aspect of your event is seamless and flawless. Our mission is simple yet impactful. To satisfy tastes while celebrating the rich tapestry of cultural and culinary preferences without any barriers of religion, caste or creed.
